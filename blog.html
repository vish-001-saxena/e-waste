Why E-Waste Awareness Matters

India generates over 2 million tonnes of e-waste annually. Only a small fraction is formally recycled. Informal processing causes severe health and environmental hazards.

  • 95% of e-waste is handled by untrained workers
  • Hazardous materials like mercury, lead, and cadmium pollute air and soil
  • Recycling can recover valuable metals and reduce mining

Public awareness is key to changing the narrative. Let's make recycling a habit.
